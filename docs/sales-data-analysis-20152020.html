1.2 Data Entry & Formulas

1.2.1 Structure in Excel

Column Description
A Region
B–G Sales (2015–2020)
H Total
I Average
J Growth %

1.2.2 Formulas Used

Calculation Formula
Total =SUM(B4:G4)
Average =AVERAGE(B4:G4)
Growth % =(G4-B4)/B4

1.2.3 Grand Total Row

Calculation Formula
Year-wise Total =SUM(B4:B7)
Overall Total =SUM(H4:H7)
Avg of Avg =AVERAGE(I4:I7)
